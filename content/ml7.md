# A compact identity built around speed and direction.

> The current mL7 identity combines a custom black wordmark with a single orange directional stroke. This page establishes the case-study structure while the original process evidence is being assembled.

- **Contribution:** Identity design
- **Documented scope:** Mark and wordmark
- **Status:** Process evidence in preparation

![The documented identity artifact, shown in full and without cropping.](media:ml7-logo-system)

## What the current artifact establishes

The wordmark is the identity rather than a neutral label beside a separate symbol. Its compressed, angular construction keeps the name readable while giving the letterforms a shared visual logic.

Black carries the primary form. Orange is reserved for one directional gesture, creating a clear hierarchy without asking a broader palette to create recognition.

## What belongs here next

The finished case study should show the original brief, rejected directions, drawing logic, spacing and scale tests, color specifications, applications, and the tradeoffs behind the selected form. Those claims will be written only when the corresponding evidence is available.

This keeps the page honest: the structure is ready, but a polished artifact is not being used as a substitute for undocumented reasoning.
