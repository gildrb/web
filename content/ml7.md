# The logo that mL7 still uses today

In 2018 I was playing a lot of competitive Overwatch and learning the game by watching people far better than me. One of them was Mihai "mL7" Lupascu. I made a logo for him as a way of saying thanks, posted it, and expected nothing back. His manager reached out privately, and the mark I designed became his identity. He still uses it today.

![mL7 logo system](media:ml7-logo-system)

### The work

I wanted the mark to carry the energy I got from watching him every day: fast, sharp, and confident. I built it as a wordmark with a clean geometric `mL` and an orange streak driving through the seven, so it reads instantly at a stream overlay's size and holds up as a profile mark and a channel banner. I iterated on the letterforms, the angle of the streak, and the balance between the black type and the accent until it felt right at every scale it needed to live at.

### What it led to

Mihai saw the work, thanked me for it, and insisted on paying me even though I had never asked. That was the first time a creator I looked up to treated my design as something worth commissioning, and it changed how seriously I took the craft. The logo has now represented his channel for years, which is the outcome I am proudest of. A mark that ships, gets adopted, and sticks around is the real test, and this one passed it.
