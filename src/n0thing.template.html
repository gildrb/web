What the current artifact establishes

The wordmark remains the constant while its internal state changes. White type and a black field remove competing visual signals, leaving timing, interruption, and reconstruction to carry the motion language.

The animation is therefore part of the identity rather than decoration applied after the mark was finished. Its behavior is the visible system demonstrated by the available artifact.

What belongs here next

The complete case study should document the original brief, static construction, rejected directions, timing studies, easing decisions, transition rules, delivery formats, and the contexts in which the animated identity was designed to operate.

Those decisions will be described when the corresponding source material is available. Until then, the page states exactly what the finished artifact demonstrates and nothing more.
